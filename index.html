What is AU-XD?

Agile processes are sweeping the software development world. Businesses no longer have the luxury of taking three years to deliver an improved product to the customer. In order to stay competitive online companies must continually adapt to the changing technologocial landscape, while at the same time being able to offer an exceptional user experience to their customers.

One of the perenial complaints about User Research is that it is too time intensive. It can take over a month to plan and conduct a traditional usability study of just a few screens. How then can a company implement agile processes while also practicing good user centered design? Through processes like story mapping, persona, rapid prototyping and dual track sprints, Scrumi UX can help your company develop a first class product discovery team that will keep your backlog full and your product centered on the needs of its core user base.
